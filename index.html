Our Mission Statement

As a team, we commit to the Scrum values of commitment, courage, focus, openness, and respect to promote collaboration, accountability, and steady progress. Our goal is to deliver a high-quality, functional product while strengthening our technical skills and teamwork in a supportive environment.

Team Rules

  • Commitment: Team members must reliably stick to deadlines
  • Courage: Proposing ideas and asking for help/clarification on essentially anything are strongly encouraged to promote a strong work ethic and everyone on the team being aligned on the same page
  • Focus: During class time and meeting times, members are expected to stay on task and complete work efficiently.
  • Openness: Clear communication is expected of each member. If anything happens that would prevent them from being able to complete their assigned tasks, they should reach out in a timely manner and plan with the others to figure out how to work around the interruption.
  • Respect: Members should maintain respectful behavior amongst other members.
